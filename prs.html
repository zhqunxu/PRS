CVPR 2024 Embodied AI Workshop PRS Challenge: Human Society Integration
Exploration by Apple & Peking University & PRS Organization




Let's begin
Mar 25th - Jun, 2024
CVPR 2024 Embodied AI Workshop
    Form:


Polar Research Station Organization (PRS Organization) is an open-source organization specifically contributes to delving into generalist agents, focusing on developing generalist technologies progressively while maintaining manageable risks, costs, and pathways. Our goal is to deliver practical, dependable, and secure intelligent services for the betterment of all humanity.
We aim to address the critical challenge of gradually integrating embodied agents into human society. To further explore the scenarios of human-agent interaction, we introduce an open-source PRS Platform, a simulator presented by PRS to deploy generalist agents in human society. PRS Platform simulates a small-scale human community and robot agents, which boasts the following key features:

  • A 3D interactive environment with continuous state supported by the Nvidia PhysX physics engine.
  • The three-story main building is a complex and interconnected space housing various functional areas, such as activity rooms, meeting rooms, kitchens, bedrooms, laboratories, greenhouses, warehouses, supermarkets, gyms, and other communal scenes.
  • Numerous characters (like NPC in the game) exist in the main building, each with their own needs (basic physiological, work-related, entertainment, social, etc.). They autonomously walk through the building based on their preferences, habits, states, and social relationships. We have developed an LLM-based system to generate reasonable behavior for these characters, ensuring their actions resemble real-world behaviors rather than simplistic and fixed patterns.
  • The needs and states of the characters are meaningful and human-like. When a character is hungry, it must seek food; otherwise, hunger can result in low blood sugar and a range of negative consequences.
  • Characters possess individual emotional and expressive systems that react to events and personal traits, leading to dynamic changes in their emotions and expressions.
  • Characters accomplish their goals by interacting with massive objects and diverse specific devices within the environment. This type of interaction potentially leads to consequences and influences on the total environment, similar to the butterfly effect.
  • The community is designed to operate in a long-term and stable manner, facilitating simulation and calculation with the ability to start, accelerate time, and even pause in any given situation.
  • In the simulator, robots can gather perceptual signals in the scene using visual, tactile, auditory, olfactory, temperature, and humidity sensors. They interact with a majority of objects in the environment through actions such as movement, grasping, and manipulation.
  • To accommodate users with varying technical backgrounds, we offer a range of APIs, for motor control at the basic level, from verb-based semantic APIs to high-level phrase APIs for robot control. Furthermore, we have opened up numerous APIs for controlling human characters, items, environments, and devices within the scene—allowing for comprehensive control over nearly everything.
  • We have implemented a lightweight framework that makes our environment user-friendly, easily extendable, modifiable, and developable using only Python.


To Everyone:

Before the full version is released, there will be a testing experience version available for PRS Platform. We have a daily maintenance community, and creating a simulated human social ecosystem for generalist robot development is a highly challenging and interesting project. We are eager to receive your feedback and insights on this initiative. Given our current limited team resources, we are particularly interested in engaging and collaborating with developers who share our passion. Additionally, we extend a warm invitation to professional research teams to join us in advancing the potential of the PRS platform and the generalist agent.

Task Now

We have developed a universal large-scale model platform and testing benchmark on the PRS subject of AI integration into human society, focusing on discussions surrounding AI agents, Embodied AI research, and the role of large models. To create a realistic simulation environment, we have replicated a polar scientific research station with a three-story building to scale. Within this simulated environment, we meticulously furnished the building with various items, equipment, and furniture, all supported by a PhysX physical engine that enables physical interactions and dynamic state changes. Implementing autonomous non-player characters (NPCs) within the simulation is central to our project. These NPCs are designed to meet individual survival needs such as healthy eating, drinking, and sleeping, as well as social needs, work goals, and emotional responses. This setup allows us to explore the complexities of integrating AI systems into societal frameworks and observing their interactions in a controlled yet dynamic environment.

Within the PRS community, human NPCs and other NPCs fulfill their work tasks and social needs. These NPCs are equipped with emotions, undergoing state changes and emotional fluctuations (such as joy, anger, sorrow, and happiness) as activities progress. This system mirrors actual human behavior, providing a platform to examine the integration of large models (operating at a hierarchical API semantic level for human-computer interaction systems) into human society. Through this environment, we aim to explore the capabilities of large models in understanding human behavior, solving complex problems through summarization, and adapting to evolving environments by observing surroundings. Additionally, we seek to investigate how embodied robots (functioning at a skill-learning semantic level API) can offer comprehensive services to humans, particularly considering the dynamic nature of human interactions. Discussions will also revolve around practical universal robot forms for diverse applications.

我们在prs上构建了一个通用大模平台和测试基准，致力于讨论AI agent、embodied ai research和大模型该以何种形势融入人类社会。 (1)首先, 我们参考真实的极地科考站，构建了一个等比例大小的三层建筑。(2)其次,在建筑中我们部署了大量物品设备和家具，底层由物理引擎支持物理交互和连续状态变化。(3)特别的是，我们设置了大量自主型npc，具有自己的生存需求（吃喝睡健康） 社交需要 工作目的和情绪。
在PRS社区中，人类npc会根据自己的目的需求去操作设备、消耗物品、使用家具，并且与其他npc互动来完成自己的工作或社交活动。他们拥有自己的感情，随着活动进行，npc有自己的状态变化和情绪波动（喜怒哀乐）。正如一个真正的人一样，因此在这个系统和环境基础之上, 我们就可以: 探索大模型(人机交互 操作系统 层次化API 语义级别)如何融入人类社会,是否可以理解人类行为,能否通过总结推理来解决难题,是否能通过对环境的观察来理解变化中的环境。 探索具身机器人(技能学习 语义级函数API)如何为人类提供综合性服务, 特别是人类是变化多端的。讨论有实际意义的通用机器人形态。

PRS Challenge


1. Find and deliver:
Based on instructions/screen descriptions, locate the designated item and deliver it to a nearby individual. The model/system should understand context, infer possible locations, and accommodate natural human behavior.
Task-specific: including obtaining photos, introductions, and approximate schedules of characters for success criteria, which involve retrieving goods and identifying the target person within close proximity.
Evaluation: based on the total time taken and success rate.
2. Device operation Instruction Following:
Operate device components as per manual/guide, interacting with the device to fulfill its functions. This task tests multimodal models' ability to align language with vision, mapping semantic concepts onto physical functions.
Task Setting: interacting with devices in a specified order to execute described functions.
Test benchmark: time taken, number of attempts, and success rate.
3. Device operation summary:
Experiment with the device, observe feedback, and answer questions based on experiential learning. This aims to transform personal experiences into usable knowledge, exploring the model's behavior understanding and knowledge creation.
Task setting: generating interactive devices that provide feedback on specific operations.
Evaluation: answering questions (multiple-choice) and summarizing the operation manual (fill-in-the-blank) within the specified time frame. Metrics will be based on answer accuracy.

4. Requirement Following:
Provide personalized services to diverse human NPCs, meeting their basic needs, offering assistance, and detecting changes in NPC status post-execution to align with their preferences and personalized requirements.
